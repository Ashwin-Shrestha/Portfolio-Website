A Research on How I made the website

While making the website, I have mainly reviewed or researched two websites, Gary Vaynerchuk's personal website and Hank Green’s personal website(Mainly, Gary V’s website because I like the black and white design). So, I have reviewed the webpages from these websites and written about it down below:

The Navigation bar

From what I have learned after looking at the websites, navigation bar is one of the most if not the most important part of a website. It helps a user to get around the website easily. So, firstly as said above, I really liked the design of black and white colour and having a hover element when the mouse is hovered near the nav bar. So, I basically created somewhat the same navigation bar with the logo at the left but my website has the nav bar on right instead of on the left as seen in his nav bar. The logo in my website when click takes a user to the home page as in his navigation bar or in some other websites.

The Portfolio/About Me

The portfolio part of hank green was one that caught my eyes while looking for websites. He had a photo on the left with a short and descriptive biography about what he does and who he is. So, I took an inspiration from his webpage and used it in mine with my photo and a short description about me.

Contact Me/CV

The CV part was mainly my own idea and I didn’t really use any webpage to make it. I just imagined about what a CV consists of and wrote it in a table and created it. However for the Contact me part, I used a website of a guy named Man Parvesh Singh Randhawa. Man Parvesh Singh Randhawa. Although his contact me form was a bit bigger than mine I created a relatively smaller contact me form but added a selection button which writes the objective of contacting me.

The Blog

The blog page was also inspired by Gary V’s blog page about Twitter. Here he writes the blog on the left side and has his advertisement on the right side as images. However, what I did was add the images related to the blog to the right side instead of in the blog itself.

The Footer

The footer I have made is a very simple one but i really wanted to make it like Hank Green's footer and many other people's footer with their social links in icons. However, I did not know how to make it and when i looked it up on youtube to learn about it, I still did not get how to make them. So,instead I made them as it is now with just my contact email-ID.
